How it works

From idea to funded project in 3 steps

A simple journey designed to take you from curious to confident.

1

Create your profile

Answer a few questions about your goals and interests. Our AI matches you with the perfect mentor based on skills, goals, and availability.

2

Build & collaborate

Work in shared workspaces with your mentor and team. Develop your idea, track milestones, attend webinars, and grow your network.

3

Launch & get funded

Present your project to the community. Launch a crowdfunding campaign and connect directly with investors and institutions.

Platform features

Everything you need to succeed

Six core features, one integrated platform.

AI-powered matchmaking

Smart pairing (0–100 score) based on shared interests, complementary skills, experience gaps, goals, and location. Switch mentors anytime.

Collaborative workspaces

Build teams, assign roles, set milestones, and develop projects together with workflow templates and goal tracking.

Crowdfunding campaigns

Set funding goals, track progress in real time, and receive investments or donations from the community and external backers.

Institution connections

Connect with universities, companies, and NGOs for internships, partnerships, and strategic funding opportunities.

Integrated scheduling

Book sessions directly in the platform with Google Calendar sync. Video calls, group sessions, and webinars — all in one place.

Safety & trust

Built-in help center, reporting tools, ratings system, and verified mentor profiles to keep every interaction safe and transparent.
